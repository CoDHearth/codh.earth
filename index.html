Covenants of the Divine Hearth

  1. We begin in Love.
  2. We honor the Living Flame.
  3. We keep the Hearth pure and tended.
  4. We trust the timing of the Deep.
  5. We speak and act with reverence.
  6. We welcome joy, grief, and mystery.
  7. We steward what is sacred.
  8. We are playful with power.
  9. We are kind to all kin.
  10. We offer what we can, when we can.
  11. We rest when we are tired.
  12. We remember who we are.
  13. We return, always, to the Hearth.
